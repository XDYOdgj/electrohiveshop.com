Keyless Entry Door Lock

This advanced keyless door lock is designed for both residential and commercial use, delivering convenience and peace of mind. With dual-sided programming, you can assign unique access codes to each side of the door, ensuring that different users have tailored entry options. The feature offers 50 user codes per side, giving you up to 5000 total combinations to meet your specific security needs.

The backlit keypad makes it easy to enter your code in low light or even in the dark, while the auto-locking function guarantees reliable operation. Perfect for families, especially those with young children, it helps prevent accidental access. Its low power consumption ensures months of use on four standard AAA batteries, and an optional USB power backup adds convenience in case of emergencies.

The design is compatible with doors up to 2-3/4" thick, making it a universal fit for various door types. It also includes a waterproof rating, ensuring durability in harsh weather conditions. A low battery alarm alerts you before the lock runs out of power, so you can stay ahead of potential issues. In addition, the synchronized mode allows both sides of the door to operate simultaneously, ensuring secure access control at all times.

With the option to pair it with our matching master key set or additional side keys, this lock adds an extra layer of versatility. Choose the silent mode to avoid unwanted noise, making it ideal for both homes and offices where discretion matters.
